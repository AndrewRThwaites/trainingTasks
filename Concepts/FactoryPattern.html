Factory Pattern

What is a factory pattern

Design patterns are the ready descriptions giving solutions to repetitive and typical project problems. They are the results of experience, hard work and a great number of trials and errors. They also represent considered and best practices of object-oriented programming (SOLID, DRY, KISS and YAGNI). The Factory Method defines a method, which should be used for creating objects instead of using a direct constructor call.
